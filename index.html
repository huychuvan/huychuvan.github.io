About me

Van-Huy Chu is currently a lecturer in the Faculty of Information Technology and Digital Economics, Banking Academy of Vietnam. He has chosen main research direction related to Robotic Process Automation (RPA). He focuses on research related to planning, building and deploying applications based on RPA platforms in the field of Vietnam higher education. Besides, he has experimentally developed a number of automation projects on RPA platforms such as UiPath, RoboCorp, akaBot,... Through his research, he aims to improve automation in management and training activities at universities in Vietnam. As a result, helping schools effectively implement digital transformation activities in universities, towards becoming a smart university.
